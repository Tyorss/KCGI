Theme Score Heatmap

테마별 매력도와 병목 강도

5점 척도입니다. valuation burden은 점수가 높을수록 부담이 큰 테마로 해석합니다.

Valuation burden is a risk score: higher means more expensive or more crowded, unlike TAM, growth, visibility, and bottleneck scores.

6~18m priority = earnings visibility + bottleneck intensity + demand momentum - valuation burden. 3~5y priority = structural growth + TAM expansion + technology transition + option value - commercialization risk.

      Scoring

      company exposure score와 source confidence score를 별도 layer로 분리.

      • Company-level revenue and operating income are not the same as market-specific revenue.
      • HBM, CoWoS, CPO, and glass-core substrate often lack company-level product operating profit disclosure.
      • Segment operating income is only shown when disclosed or mechanically calculated from disclosed segment margin.
      • Do not infer HBM operating profit from HBM market share unless a separate estimate source is added.
      • CoWoS allocation is not market share. CPO and glass-core substrate should be shown as roadmap/status markets.

      Data caveats: TAM values are based on heterogeneous public estimates and should not be interpreted as directly comparable without checking market definition. Enterprise SSD 2030 value and aggressive CPU TAM are scenario estimates.
